The smallest size of the logo should be a 50px in height, in order to preserve legibility. Alternative versions of the logo include a rich black version, a reversed version, and several one color options in either The Greater Change green or energy bird green

Safe Area

The minimum safety area is calculated by the space X as shown above, surrounding the logotype. This will allow enough space around the logo and avoid overcrowding, and keep the clean integrity of the logo in tact. The logo should never be placed on top of a busy pattern background.

IDENTITY COLOR

The primary identifying color for The Greater Change is known as Energy Bird Green and should be used where an item needs to be accented. This color is to be paired with the secondary and product colors as needed.

MESSAGING

The voice and vibe for The Greater Change should be kept fun and simple. The Greater Change doesn’t take itself seriously and projects a creative tone in its messaging. The most important thing to consider when writing messaging for The Greater Change is to have fun with it.
